Empathy Pen

Welcome to Empathy Pen, a website developed by Team Teaching Empathy!

Our goal is to connect you with people with different viewpoints. Too often, we seek out interactions with like-minded individuals. In order to understand those who are different than us, we need to make conscious efforts to get to know them.

Step 1: Sign up, and indicate your current viewpoints on controversial issues. Decide on which issues you wish to "learn about the other side."

Step 2: Get matches! You'll be matched with those who think differently than you, but who, like you, want to understand opposing viewpoints.

Step 3: Engage in a guided conversation. We will facilitate a discussion between you and your pen pal on the issues in which you differ.
